About Me

My name is Katherine, and I'm learning full stack web deveopment. In my current work as a business analyst, I use SQL and Hive on a daily basis.

My undergrad background is in philosophy, and I never thought I'd be a programmer. But when I started teaching myself SQL and Python a few years ago, I was hooked! To me, programming is like math, or learning a new language.

I'm excited to learn more front and back-end technologies, especially Javascript & MongoDB. My ultimate career goal is to be a back-end developer.

Outside of work & programming, I'm an avid backpacker & runner. Now that I'm getting comfortable with HTML and CSS, I'm interested in creating a website where I could post backpacking trip reports and photos.
